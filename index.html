Ancient Artifact: The Golden Mask

The Golden Mask of Pharaoh is an exquisite artifact dating back to 1323 BC. This stunning piece was discovered in the tomb of the ancient Egyptian king, showcasing intricate craftsmanship and a glimpse into the afterlife beliefs of the civilization.

Interesting Facts:

  • Made from 11 kg of solid gold.
  • Inlaid with semi-precious stones and lapis lazuli.
  • Symbolizes divine protection and immortality.
  • Found in the tomb of King Tutankhamun in 1922.
